Thank you for installing the crypto-app chart.

To verify that everything is running:

1. Check pods:

   kubectl get pods

2. Check services:

   kubectl get svc

To access the frontend from your browser (ClusterIP service):

1. Port-forward the frontend service:

   kubectl port-forward svc/frontend-service 5002:5002

2. Open the browser at:

   http://localhost:5002

The frontend will call the backend at "backend-service:5001", and the backend will use MySQL at "mysqldb:3306" with the credentials from values.yaml.
